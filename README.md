## **Theory**

Prompt engineering refers to the **art of designing effective prompts** that guide AI models to generate desired outputs.
Well-structured prompts use patterns that improve focus, creativity, factual accuracy, and stylistic consistency.

Different writing tasks (like reports, case studies, or stories) require **different prompting strategies**:

| **Prompt Pattern**      | **Description**                                                  | **Use Case Example**                                         | **Effect on Output**                         |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Query Decomposition** | Breaks complex questions into smaller sub-queries                | ‚ÄúExplain causes, effects, and solutions for air pollution.‚Äù  | Improves clarity and logical flow            |
| **Decision Making**     | Lets AI select among multiple narrative or analytical directions | ‚ÄúChoose between positive or tragic ending and justify it.‚Äù   | Enhances creativity and reasoning            |
| **Answer Engineering**  | Controls structure, format, and tone of the response             | ‚ÄúUse a formal tone, 5 sections, and bullet points for data.‚Äù | Produces structured and polished output      |
| **Fact Checklist**      | Encourages factual verification before responding                | ‚ÄúEnsure data aligns with 2025 projections.‚Äù                  | Increases accuracy and reliability           |
| **Tail Generation**     | Asks AI to extend or refine an existing idea logically           | ‚ÄúContinue the story keeping tone consistent.‚Äù                | Ensures continuity and coherence             |
| **Menu Actions**        | Provides predefined content directions                           | ‚ÄúChoose to write as a scientist, teacher, or journalist.‚Äù    | Gives varied and role-based perspectives     |
| **Semantic Filtering**  | Applies tone, style, or audience filters                         | ‚ÄúRewrite this paragraph for a high-school audience.‚Äù         | Aligns language and tone with target readers |

## **Procedure**

1. Identify two distinct content genres‚Äîone **technical** and one **creative**.

3. Record and analyze the improvement in tone, structure, and quality after each refinement.
4. Observe how prompt structure directly influences the nature and clarity of AI responses.
5. Document the final outputs and observations.

## **Prompting and Results**

---

### **Scenario 1 ‚Äì Technical Case Study**

#### **Step 1 ‚Äì Simple Prompt**

> ‚ÄúWrite a case study on AI in waste management.‚Äù

**Output Summary:**

* Very brief and surface-level content.
* Lacked numerical data, structure, and region-specific details.
* Tone was informative but not analytical.

---

#### **Step 2 ‚Äì Using Query Decomposition & Answer Engineering**

> ‚ÄúWrite a detailed case study on the use of AI and IoT in smart waste management.
> Divide it into: *Introduction, Problem Definition, Technology Used, Implementation, Results, and Future Scope.*
> Maintain an academic tone with real-world references.‚Äù

**Output Summary:**

* Added sections with logical transitions.
* Mentioned AI-based bin sensors, route optimization, and waste segregation.
* Tone improved to match professional reporting.

---

#### **Step 3 ‚Äì Using Semantic Filtering & Fact Checklist**

> ‚ÄúRevise the report to include realistic data from 2025 urban technology studies.
> Add examples from developing countries.
> Ensure factual consistency and include at least one measurable metric.‚Äù

## **6. Discussion**

This experiment illustrates that **prompt engineering is a decisive factor** in generating high-quality, domain-specific AI outputs.

* **Analytical Content (Reports/Case Studies):**
  Benefits most from *query decomposition*, *answer engineering*, and *fact verification*.
  It ensures factual consistency, structured format, and clarity.

* **Creative Content (Stories/Poems):**
  Benefits most from *decision-making*, *semantic filtering*, and *tail generation*, enabling emotional resonance and stylistic control.

* The combination of prompt techniques creates **hybrid content** ‚Äî e.g., educational storytelling, journalistic fiction, and data-infused essays.

Hence, the **art of prompt design = creative direction + structural logic**.
